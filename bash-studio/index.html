Bash Studio

Description

Bash Studio is an online shop that sells custom printable party invitations, accessories, and decor. Bash was opened in 2012 and has since had over 1,000 sales, over 100 positive reviews, and has been featured on various wedding and party planning blogs.

Work Completed
